# mental-health-support-platform
A lightweight mental health support platform built during a 24‑hour hackathon to provide quick, accessible, and stigma‑free emotional assistance using AI.

##  Problem Statement
Millions of people lack access to mental health resources due to cost, distance, and stigma. This platform offers instant emotional support, especially for underserved communities.

## Solution

Our solution is a simple and accessible mental health support platform designed for people who cannot easily reach professional help. The platform focuses on providing quick emotional support, basic guidance, and self‑help tools in a private and user‑friendly way.
